Spoofing Attacks

Spoofing is a method for attackers to decieve companies or homes by stealing information, and it is a huge cyberattack if the attacker can successfully hack into a home or company, If you are a victim of this cyberattack you are at a huge risk. Here are some of the many spoofing attacks that could potentially be targeted towards you.

  1. IP Spoofing

    • Denial of Service (DoS) Attack
    • Man in the Middle

  2. URL Spoofing

    • Intrusion

  3. Email Spoofing

    • Cause confusion or discredit a person
    • Social Engineering (phishing)
    • Hide identity of the sender (spamming)
